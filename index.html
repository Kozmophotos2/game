Personalisation

Sparx Maths creates an hour's worth of perfectly tailored practice homework for each student each week driven by your school’s scheme of learning.

The practice is challenging, to ensure students need to think and, crucially, achievable so that students can be successful.

We also ensure the practice uses spaced repetition and interleaving to support a change in students' long-term memories.

We are proud that Sparx Maths is inclusive and can adapt to all students' current level of expertise. This promotes engagement, confidence and ultimately learning.
